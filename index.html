My Plants

  • 3rd semester group exam project
  • Development of a wep application concept
  • HTML, CSS and JavaScript
  • Firebase for data storage

The idea with "My Plants" was to create a web app where the user could get an overview over their plants at home and how to take care of those.
With Firebase we made it possible to make a personal profile, that storages your data.
In the plant library you can search and (in the future) scan the plants you have at home wich you can add to your own "My Plants"-profile. Here you get information about each plant, that tells you when and how much to water each plant.
In the future we would like to develop the calendar and notifications functions.
